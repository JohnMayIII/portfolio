I spent my first two years of highschool at the Tougaloo Early College Highschool before transferring over to the Mississippi School of Mathematics and Science. Despite a rough start to my tenure there due to COVID-19, it was great place to be. During junior year, I was asked to fill in for a sort of theatrical production called the Eight of May Emancipation Celebration in which I took on the role of store owner Allen Rabb and told his story.

When my senior year started, I talked to people a lot more and helped guide a group of juniors through their first year along with some fellow seniors. They would end up teaching me how to skateboard as a kind of reciprocation. I would go on to enlist their help from time to time with projects including a Shakespeare-related project using a media of my choosing. The image of me with a sword in my hand was a test photo for various angles before taking the actual pictures.

I finished highschool in May 2022 and sought to be a part of the Mississippi Coding Academy. I am here now and will complete the 11 months before I go off to attend college in the fall of 2023.
